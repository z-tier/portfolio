Game Development

VECTOR-7 — A Sentient Gun Roguelike

Originally conceived during a jam team project that didn’t quite land — but the idea stuck. Now rebuilt from the ground up as a chaotic top-down shooter where you recoil to move. Built in Godot with modular upgrades and (ideally) randomized maps. Follow the development on my Discord to see it evolve.

Genre: Roguelike, Action | Role: Solo Dev | Status: In Development

Last One Standing — Shoot, Solve, Survive

A calculation-themed shooter made for Mini Jam 183. Shoot numbers and operations to solve equations — when your result hits zero, selected enemies explode and you score points. Fast, strategic, and weirdly educational.

Genre: Puzzle Shooter | Role: Solo Dev | Status: Completed?

Pathogenos — Infect to Conquer

My entry for Godot Wild Jam 79, and also my first release. Play as a parasite infecting a new host. Spread your infection through red blood cells while evading immunity responses. Infect the brain to win. Currently planning a full remake with expanded mechanics.
